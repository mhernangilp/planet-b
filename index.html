DEMO ABOUT US

Transform the Unknown

Welcome to Planet-B, the game where the survival of humanity rests in your hands! Earth’s resources are nearly depleted, and our species faces extinction. Your mission? To discover and transform a new home, Planet-B. Embark on a journey across the galaxy in search of the perfect exoplanet. Once you land, you’ll explore its mysteries, protect it from dangers, and gradually shape it into a place where life can thrive. Designed for players aged fourteen and up, Planet-B challenges your strategy and creativity with daily tasks.

Using real data from NASA’s Exoplanet Archive, Planet-B evaluates each planet’s potential for life by analyzing its similarity to Earth and atmospheric conditions. Unlock minigames to boost your planet's stats and overcome challenges as you work to secure humanity’s future. Ready to embark on this exciting journey filled with fun and learning?
